WORKING:

Connect the modules as shown in the diagram or as given in the connections text file.

Install the apk file given on any android device and connect to the bluetooth module first from android settings.

Open the app and turn on/off 1st or 2nd device according to your need.

**If the Tx led on Arduino is not lighting up while pressing on/off button in the app then change the wires connecting Rx and Tx pins of HC-05 to Arduino.
